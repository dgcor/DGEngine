Griswold speaks of The Anvil of Fury - a
legendary artifact long searched for,
but never found. Crafted from the
metallic bones of the Razor Pit demons,
the Anvil of Fury was smelt around the
skulls of the five most powerful magi of
the underworld. Carved with runes of
power and chaos, any weapon or armor
forged upon this Anvil will be immersed
into the realm of Chaos, imbedding it with
magical properties. It is said that the
unpredictable nature of Chaos makes it
difficult to know what the outcome of
this smithing will be...
